Meet The Team

We recognize that all patients are unique and deserve to receive comprehensive dental care that reflects their individual needs. Our team of experienced dentists is committed to working with you and your family to create a comfortable, stress-free, and rewarding dental experience every time you visit our practice. With a focus on delivering the highest quality of care, our skilled professionals offer a wide range of dental services to cater to your specific requirements.

As advocates of comprehensive dental services, we strive to go beyond the conventional to ensure that you and your family receive the exceptional care you deserve. From the moment you step into our office, our dedicated team will work diligently to make your visit a positive and comfortable one.

Dr. Arthur Greyf

Dr. Arthur Greyf is honored to give people beautiful smiles while restoring their dental health and function.

Since opening his practice in 2000, Dr. Greyf has continually incorporated the latest high-tech equipment and advanced techniques into his patient care. This allows him to deliver amazing results in the shortest time frame.

By providing the best of modern dentistry, in a welcoming and service-oriented environment, he ensures his patients achieve the results they deserve: optimal oral health and smiles they love to share!

Learn more

Karen

I love meeting so many wonderful people and sharing my passion for dentistry. It is my pleasure to help create a calm and welcoming environment for patients where they can be cared for by our skilled, compassionate doctors and support staff.

Karen has been in the dental field for over 30 years and has done almost everything in a dental office from reception, chairside-assisting, insurance, treatment planning, and finance. She is bringing her knowledge and experience to train and support the staff.

When not at work, Karen enjoys vegan cooking & baking and photography. She loves spending time with her family, especially when attending live music events, plays, and art exhibits.

Iryna

Iryna is originally from Ukraine and has been working in the dental field and by Dr. Greyf's side for over 20 years. She is a highly trained registered dental assistant who takes pride in staying current with continuing dental education classes, as a commitment to helping and caring for the patient's dental health and comfort. She enjoys cooking, reading, and spending time with her grandchildren in her free time. She is looking forward to meeting you soon!

Farkhod

«Dentistry is my passion. I enjoy helping people to overcome their fear of dentistry, teach them about the importance of oral health, and give them the opportunity to smile with confidence».

Farkhod recently moved to the U.S. from Uzbekistan where he graduated from Uzbekistan State University of Dentistry. He came to learn rapidly changing dental technology. Currently, he is working as a dental assistant while going to school to get his license in the states.

When not at work he spends a lot of time traveling and fishing. His hobby is to take pictures of different styles of architecture. He is always open to new experiences and adventures.

Viktoriia

Viktoriia recently moved to the United States from Ukraine. She really enjoys working with patients, and likes seeing the wonderful smile makeovers. Victoria is currently working as a dental assistant while going to school to continue her education to become an attorney. In her free time, she enjoys sports, traveling, watching movies and spending time with her fiancee.

Emely

Emely is often the first friendly face our new patients meet, aiming to make everyone feel welcomed, understood, and cared for from the start. Originally from Ecuador, she holds a BA in Translation and completed three years of dental studies before moving to New Jersey. She later earned a BS in Science from Montclair State University. This diverse background helps her connect with patients, especially those feeling nervous, and explain dental care clearly and reassuringly.

Fully bilingual in Spanish and English, Emely takes pride in supporting patients from diverse backgrounds. She assists new patients with scheduling, paperwork, and questions to ensure a smooth, stress-free first visit. Emely is passionate about dentistry and hopes to become a dentist one day. Outside work, she enjoys traveling and spending time with family.

Katerina

Katerina, is originally from Riga, Latvia. She relocated to New Jersey in 2008 from Connecticut and has been working with Dr. Greyf since and never looked back! In her role as Office Manager, she has found love for both the clinical side of helping others achieve their dental health and smile goals, as well as the administrative side of work with the practice's fantastic patients. She absolutely adores the amazing team who has become her work family. She loves to travel and spend time outdoors. She enjoys cooking and spending fun quality time with her family.

Zhanna

'Being able to smile confidently makes a great impact on people«s lives and I am happy that I can help».

Zhanna is a successful dentist from Ukraine. She and her family moved to the United States when the war started back home. Currently she is working as a dental assistant while going to school to get her dental license. She loves her job and learning new things in dentistry. She takes pride in and enjoys making positive changes in people's lives.

She also enjoys sports like acrobatics and gymnastics, and spends a lot of time reading and traveling.

Melissa

Hello, Melissa here! I'm your New Patient Coordinator, dedicated to making your first experience with our practice comfortable and informative. Since 2015, I’ve worked in the dental field and hold a certification in Dental Radiology, giving me a solid understanding of office operations and patient care. I spend much of my time on the phone, listening to patients’ concerns and helping those interested in dental implants. My background in customer service, including family business and as a flight attendant, helps me anticipate needs and offer great support.

It’s very rewarding to hear the happiness in a patient’s voice when they find the right solution, and I’m here to guide you throughout the process. I’m also fluent in Brazilian Portuguese. Outside work, I enjoy crafts, traveling, and spending time with my family.
