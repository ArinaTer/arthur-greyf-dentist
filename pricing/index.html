What Makes Us Different

We do All on X

We anchor full dentures with 5–6 implants for superior comfort and stability. Most offices use All-on-4, which relies on only 4 implants.

We have better technology

First in NJ with Yomi® Robotic-Assisted Tech. Yomi® tech ensures precise implant placement with smaller incisions and no stitches, leading to faster, more comfortable healing and longer-lasting implants.

We speak multiple languages

Did you know we speak multiple languages? English, Russian, Ukrainian, Spanish, Yiddish all fluently and some French, Italian and Portuguese, too!

We care about you

We want you to have the new smile of your dreams! Our doctors and staff are renowned for really listening to our patients and going the extra mile! We look forward to you joining our dental family!
