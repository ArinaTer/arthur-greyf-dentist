ALL ON X IMPLANT-SUPPORTED DENTURES

Painless. Fast. Professional.

Enjoy secure, natural, hassle-free implant-supported dentures for a fast, confident smile.

Book a free consultation

Yomi robotic system makes implant supported dentures faster than ever

With the implementation of the Yomi Robotic System for implant placement, as well as the state-of-the-art digital design software available at our office, it is now easier and faster than ever to provide patients with implant-supported dentures. Depending on if it's a single or dual arch procedure, your implants can be placed and your temporary denture can be 3D-printed and fitted to the implants in just a few short hours!

Our team is also happy to offer same-day denture conversion; if you already have a removable implant denture, we are able to convert it to a fixed denture in one day! No more taking your dentures out to clean them or to sleep.

Why you should install our implants

We use the Yomi Robotic System — a breakthrough in dental implant technology that helps your doctor place implants with unmatched accuracy, safety, and care.

  • Millimeter-level precision using 3D scans
  • Safer surgery, even with bone loss or complex anatomy
  • Less invasive, faster recovery
  • Robot assists, doctor stays in full control
  • Fewer complications, better long-term results

If you're experiencing tooth pain, especially when chewing, or increased sensitivity to hot or cold, your tooth’s nerve might be damaged. This could be caused by deep decay or a fracture. If left untreated, the infection can spread, potentially leading to tooth loss.

Root canal therapy removes the infected tissue, cleans the root canals, and seals them to prevent further infection. Afterward, the tooth is restored with a filling or crown to maintain its shape and function. This helps preserve the tooth and relieve pain.

Our Technology

We use state-of-the-art technology to ensure your treatment is precise, effective, and as comfortable as possible. These technologies help us provide the best possible care with greater efficiency and precision.

  • The Root ZX Apex Locator helps our doctors accurately measure the root canal length, ensuring precise cleaning and sealing.
  • Digital X-rays provide fast, accurate, and low-radiation imaging to evaluate the tooth’s condition.
  • Rotary Endodontics utilizes modern tools that allow for faster and more efficient cleaning of the root canals, reducing trauma to the tooth.

Materials Used

We use only the highest quality materials.

  • Dental composites are safe and reliable materials used to fill the root canal after treatment.
  • Porcelain crowns are durable and natural-looking, designed to restore your tooth after the procedure.
  • Top brands ensure the longevity and quality of your dental restoration, as we only work with trusted manufacturers.

Your First Visit

  • We will examine the tooth and take X-rays to assess the situation.
  • We'll discuss possible treatment options and create a personalized treatment plan.
  • You will have the opportunity to ask questions and understand all aspects of the procedure.

The initial consultation costs $N, and this amount will be credited toward the cost of treatment if you decide to proceed with root canal therapy.

Treatment Steps

The procedure typically takes 1-2 hours, and you may need a follow-up visit for the permanent restoration.

Consultation and Diagnosis

We assess your tooth, take X-rays, and determine the extent of the damage.

Numbing and Isolation

Local anesthesia ensures comfort, and a dental dam keeps the tooth dry during treatment.

Accessing and Cleaning the Tooth

A small opening is made to remove infected tissue and clean the root canals.

Sealing the Canals

The canals are sealed with a biocompatible material to prevent reinfection.

Restoration

A filling or crown is placed to restore the tooth's function and appearance.
