Our Technology

We use state-of-the-art technology to ensure your treatment is precise, effective, and as comfortable as possible. These technologies help us provide the best possible care with greater efficiency and precision.

  • The Root ZX Apex Locator helps our doctors accurately measure the root canal length, ensuring precise cleaning and sealing.
  • Digital X-rays provide fast, accurate, and low-radiation imaging to evaluate the tooth’s condition.
  • Rotary Endodontics utilizes modern tools that allow for faster and more efficient cleaning of the root canals, reducing trauma to the tooth.

Materials Used

We use only the highest quality materials.

  • Dental composites are safe and reliable materials used to fill the root canal after treatment.
  • Porcelain crowns are durable and natural-looking, designed to restore your tooth after the procedure.
  • Top brands ensure the longevity and quality of your dental restoration, as we only work with trusted manufacturers.

Your First Visit

  • We will examine the tooth and take X-rays to assess the situation.
  • We'll discuss possible treatment options and create a personalized treatment plan.
  • You will have the opportunity to ask questions and understand all aspects of the procedure.
