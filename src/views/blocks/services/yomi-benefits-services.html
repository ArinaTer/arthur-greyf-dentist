Yomi robotic system makes implant supported dentures faster than ever

With the implementation of the Yomi Robotic System for implant placement, as well as the state-of-the-art digital design software available at our office, it is now easier and faster than ever to provide patients with implant-supported dentures. Depending on if it's a single or dual arch procedure, your implants can be placed and your temporary denture can be 3D-printed and fitted to the implants in just a few short hours!

Our team is also happy to offer same-day denture conversion; if you already have a removable implant denture, we are able to convert it to a fixed denture in one day! No more taking your dentures out to clean them or to sleep.

Why you should install our implants

We use the Yomi Robotic System — a breakthrough in dental implant technology that helps your doctor place implants with unmatched accuracy, safety, and care.

  • Millimeter-level precision using 3D scans
  • Safer surgery, even with bone loss or complex anatomy
  • Less invasive, faster recovery
  • Robot assists, doctor stays in full control
  • Fewer complications, better long-term results
