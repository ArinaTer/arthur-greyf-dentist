Are You a Candidate?

You may be a candidate for full-mouth reconstruction when any of the following apply to you.

  • Your teeth have been lost due to decay or trauma
  • Your teeth have been injured or fractured
  • Your teeth have become severely worn from long-term acid erosion caused by foods, beverages, or acid reflux
  • You experience ongoing jaw, muscle, or headache pain related to your bite
