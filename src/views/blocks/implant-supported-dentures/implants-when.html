You might need an implant if you:

Patients who opt for implant-supported dentures are more confident when they talk, laugh, and eat in public. You'll never have to worry about your false teeth slipping or falling out while you eat or are engaged in conversation.

  • Lost a tooth due to injury or decay
  • Have uncomfortable or unstable dentures
  • Want a permanent solution, not a quick fix

Why patients love implants:

Implant-supported dentures are far more comfortable because they are secure and feel like natural teeth. There is also less irritation of gum tissue; patients who have conventional dentures often suffer pain and canker sores.

  • Look, feel, and function like natural teeth
  • Last for decades with proper care
  • No damage to neighboring teeth
  • Let you eat, laugh, and speak with full confidence
