Your New Smile, Step by Step

Implants are placed in the jaw, where they are anchored firmly in the surrounding bone. Attachments are placed in the underside of the denture that connect to the implants. When placed in the mouth, the denture snaps onto the implants, keeping it stable and secure.

Scan & Plan

We take a detailed 3D scan and plan your implant digitally

Guided Placement

Using robotic assistance, your doctor places the implant precisely and safely

Healing & Integration

The implant bonds with your jawbone for a secure, long-lasting foundation

New Tooth, New You

A beautiful crown is placed — designed to look and feel just like your real tooth
