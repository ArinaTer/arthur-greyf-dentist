Yomi robotic system makes implant supported dentures faster than ever

With the implementation of the Yomi Robotic System for implant placement, as well as the state-of-the-art digital design software available at our office, it is now easier and faster than ever to provide patients with implant-supported dentures. Depending on if it's a single or dual arch procedure, your implants can be placed and your temporary denture can be 3D-printed and fitted to the implants in just a few short hours!

Our team is also happy to offer same-day denture conversion; if you already have a removable implant denture, we are able to convert it to a fixed denture in one day! No more taking your dentures out to clean them or to sleep.

Speak, Eat, and Smile Freely

Confidence in Social Situations

Patients who opt for implant-supported dentures are more confident when they talk, laugh, and eat in public. You'll never have to worry about your false teeth slipping or falling out while you eat or are engaged in conversation.

Superior Comfort and Gum Health

Implant-supported dentures are far more comfortable because they are secure and feel like natural teeth. There is also less irritation of gum tissue; patients who have conventional dentures often suffer pain and canker sores.

Natural Appearance and Bone Preservation

Implant-supported dentures look more natural than conventional dentures because less plastic is needed for retention. Wearing implant-supported dentures can also help your appearance in the long term by preventing jaw shrinkage and bone loss.

Clear and Easy Speech

Patients with conventional dentures have difficulty with speaking clearly. Slurred speech and clicking noises are common problems. Implant-supported dentures can help you relax and talk easily with a more clear and natural voice.

No Adhesives or Removal Needed

Best of all, with implant-supported dentures, there is no need to use denture adhesives or take them out to sleep.

Need implants?

Whether you've lost one tooth or all of them, implants offer a natural-looking, long-term solution — without the drawbacks of dentures or bridges.
